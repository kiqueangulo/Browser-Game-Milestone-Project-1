HERE WE GO!

Rhymeable Knowledge is about guessing words that rhyme with a random or chosen word (the guide word), having their definition, typo of word, and one letter uncover as clues.

LEVELS

Each word has three(3) levels. Level 1: you will have 5 words (rhymes) to guess; level 2: you will have 3 words to guess; and finally, level 3: you will have 1 word to guess. The difficulty increases as you advance through levels.

ATTEMPS

You will count with 5 lives (represented with hearts). To attemps represent 1 heart, and your lives goes from level 1 to level 3 for each guide word. Therefore, you have ten opportunities per word to complete its 3 levels.

HOME COMMANDS

If you click PLAY, a random guide word will be chosen for you to start playing. On the other hand, If you click WORDS, you can choose any word you want from our database.
